How to Cook a Delicious Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Ingredients:

  • Broccoli: Fresh Broccoli heads roughly chopped into small florets
  • Carrots: Diced into thin half moons or shredded
  • Aromatics: Garlic and Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese: Extra Sharp and pre-shredded
  • Dairy: Half and Half rewards this soup with the perfect creamy flavor & texture!
  • Seasoning: Ground Black Pepper, Paprika, Dijon mustard paste, and salt
  • Roux: All purpose flour + unsalted butter
  • Liquid: Bouillon dissolved in warm water
    		•

    Instructions:

    1. Saute aromatics & make the roux: Saute the onion + garlic over NORMAL heat settings on SAUTE function. Add the flour (only if you own an older model Instant Pot, otherwise skip the flour now) and saute for 30 seconds.
    2. Pressure cook broccoli: Add carrots and all the seasonings. Mix well and also de-glaze the pot very well scraping the sides and the bottom of the pot with a spatula to loosen any stuck food particles. This will prevent the BURN error and itâ€™s a very important step!
    3. Add dairy and cheese: Add the cream. Mix well. Grab a potato masher and mash a few cooked brocolis if you prefer a soup with added body but still chunky. Turn off the instant pot. Add the cheease in 2-3 batches and stir well with a spatula to help incorporate and melt the cheese very well into the soup.

    Storing Tips

    You can store the pot of the broccoli cheddar
    soup for upto 3 days in the refrigerator. Always use clean and dry air tight containers.
